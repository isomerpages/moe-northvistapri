The Malay Cultural Club aims to promote the extensive use of Malay Language through culture programmes and instill the love for Malay Language in students.Â Students are also given the opportunity to learn and improve their Malay Language through the yearly Speech and Drama workshop. Selected students may be invited to showcase their talent in acting through Drama and Storytelling competitions.

#### Recruitment
*   Students from Primary 2 to Primary 5 who show interest in the CCA are welcomed to be part of the Malay Cultural Club.
*   Students who are proficient in the use of Malay Language and have talent in stage performance will be recommended by the Malay teachers to join the Malay Cultural Club.
