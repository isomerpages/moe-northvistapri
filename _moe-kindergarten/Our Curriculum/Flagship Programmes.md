<h4>Flagship Programmes</h4>
<p>The children embark on an exciting learning journey through the two flagship
programmes, the <strong>HI-Light Programme</strong> and <strong>Starlight Literacy Programme</strong> for
English Language (EL) and Mother Tongue Languages (MTL). In addition, the
children will also engage in a <strong>Weeks of Wonder(WoW)</strong> project
each term.</p>
